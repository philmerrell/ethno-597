The Email feature

Email is still a common, relevant and sometimes even preferred channel of communication. Email is often a "noisy" channel and can be difficult to compete for student's attention. However, this doesn't mean we shouldn't target students through this form of communication.

Ideally we can provide beautiful mobile-friendly templates and provide senders with the analytics to view how successful their message is.
