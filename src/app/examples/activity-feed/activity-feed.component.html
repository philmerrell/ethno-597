Activity Feed Explanation

The activity feed feature is analagous to a newsfeed. Similar to applications such as Facebook, Twitter, and news apps, its goal is to present fresh and timely content that is personalized and should encourage students to check for new information regularly.

Content in the feed is ephemeral in nature. Items are pushed down the feed as newer items appear. It should contain “want-to-know” or “should-know” information for students.

Message priority: Low Medium

Message relevance: Medium High

Examples:
  • Campus update articles
  • BSPR content
  • Sports
  • Campus Events
  • Social Media Posts
  • Timely Reminders
  • Upcoming Academic Calendar Items
