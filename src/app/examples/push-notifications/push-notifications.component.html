Push Notification Explanation

The push notification feature

It should contain high priority “want-to-know” or “should-know” information for students. This form of communication should be highly relevant to each individual. Push notifications should direct students to the mobile app to take direct action.

Message priority: Medium to High

Message relevance: High

Examples: Semester grade posted, registration appointment starts now, You have a todo, You have a hold, Tuition due tomorrow
