SMS Explanation

The SMS feature

It should contain high priority “should-know” or “have-to-know” information for students. Text messages are a very direct form of communication and should most likely be considered an opt-in feature. Text messages are currently used at Boise State as a part of the Bronco Alert system. Other forms of communication such as chatbots and lead following

Message priority: Medium to High

Message relevance: High

Examples:
  • Advising chatbot (for Max)
  • All First Year students to remind of Required Advising Hold
  • Students with appointments in Advising and Academic Support Center (like a dentist appointment)
  • At-risk students
