Innovation Sweet Spot

Desirability

A number of interviews with various potential stakeholders were conducted. These interviews ranged from faculty, to staff at various administrative levels. The key takeaway from this body of interviews is that this functionality is desired and each interview provided a wealth of information on potential use cases. Through these interviews I was also able to categorize the stakeholders in to categories represented in "personas".

Feasibility

The key technology for this application is provided by Amazon Web Services's Simple Notification Service (SNS). SNS is an API for subscribe and publish messaging. It helps to coordinate the delivery of messages to subscribing endpoints and clients. SNS is flexible in how it can be used and provides a variety of communication channels.

AWS: Simple Notification Service

Viability

There are four primary channels to engage students with on a mobile device. They are: Push Notifications, SMS, Email, and what we are calling the Newsfeed. Each channel carries norms for what kind of messaging is deemed appropriate. Different levels of relevance and priority can dictate the most appropriate channel to send messages through.

Customer Journey Map

The first phase of this study will be primarily concerned with interviewing stakeholders at Boise State and will explore four primary questions:

  • "Who do you communicate with?"
  • "At what particular time of year?"
  • "Through what means?"
  • "With what priority?"

Ultimately I would like to understand how and what various stakeholders on campus communicate to students in order to better inform campus needs. This communication tool would provide contextually relevant communication individualized to the student and a the most ideal time.

The current version of this application represents the accumluation of work for the Spring 2018 semester toward an interdisciplary Master's degree at Boise State University. Here I explore potential solutions through proof of concept demos allowing administrators to send targeted communications to student mobile devices.

I want myBoiseState Mobile to be more focused on helping students navigate milestones, and important dates, and facilitate targeted communication. Less about providing links as an SSO hub.
