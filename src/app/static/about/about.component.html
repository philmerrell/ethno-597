This application explores potential solutions through proof of concept demos allowing administrators to send targeted communications to students through an application on their mobile device. This would allow various administrative stakeholders (instructors, chairs, Deans, etc) to engage with students when it matters mostâ€“their phone.

The work represented here is the result of a design ethnography course at Boise State University (ETHNO 597: Fundamentals of Design Ethnography). The methods used to design these high-fidelity mock-ups were customer journey mapping, empathy maps, and stakeholder interviews.
