About

This application is the result of a design ethnography course at Boise State University (ETHNO 597: Fundamentals of Design Ethnography). It explores ideas and potential solutions through proof of concept demos allowing administrators to send targeted communications to students through an application on their mobile device. The methods used to design these high-fidelity mock-ups were customer journey mapping, empathy maps, and stakeholder interviews.
