Stakeholder Personas

Administrative Level

Examples: Provost, Deans, VPs, Director
Needs: More refined methods and flexibility for communicating with wide variety of students.
Responsibilities: Oversee educational opportunities that deepen and enrich the undergraduate experience, student recruitment
Goals: Communicate with campus community, Retention, Connect students to campus opportunity and other high-level messaging.
Concerns: Concerned with how this scales to outside groups. Doesn't want to our institution to abuse the priviledge with students.

Academic Support Staff

Examples: College and major-level support staff, Advising, OIT, Student Involvement
Needs: Methods for targeting particular groups for support and status messages
Responsibilities: Support student success through majors, events and programs, and technology.
Goals: Student engagement
